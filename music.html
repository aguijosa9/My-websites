Faye Webster

Andrea Guijosa


Faye Webster is an Atlanta-based indie folk singer-songwriter known for her deeply personal lyrics, blending country, Americana, R&B, and hip-hop influences, often featuring pedal steel guitar. Raised in a musical family, she began writing songs at 14, released her debut album at 16, and later gained acclaim for her genre-defying sound and acclaimed albums like Atlanta Millionaires Club (2019) and I Know I'm Funny haha (2021)
